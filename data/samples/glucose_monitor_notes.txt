Project: GlucoTrack Pro

We're building a Bluetooth-enabled continuous glucose monitor for adults with Type 1 and Type 2 diabetes. The sensor is worn on the upper arm for up to 14 days.

Intended use: The GlucoTrack Pro system is intended for continuous measurement of interstitial fluid glucose levels in adults (18+) with diabetes. Data is transmitted via Bluetooth Low Energy to a companion mobile app (iOS/Android) for display and trend analysis.

Technology: Electrochemical glucose oxidase sensor, BLE 5.0 radio, rechargeable transmitter pod. Patient-contacting materials include medical-grade adhesive and PET substrate.

Software: Mobile app displays glucose values, alerts, and trend arrows. Cloud sync optional. IEC 62304 Class B software.
